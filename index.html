Welcome to My Cyberbullying Awareness Website

This website helps people understand what cyberbullying is and how we can stop it.

What Is Cyberbullying?

Cyberbullying is when someone uses the internet or phones to hurt, tease, or scare others.
It can happen through messages, pictures, videos, or even social media posts.

Why This Website?

This site explains the causes, effects, how to stop cyberbullying, and how to get help.
